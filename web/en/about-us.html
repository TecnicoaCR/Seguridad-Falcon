About us

"To serve and protect"

Falcon Alarm Monitoring CR S.A., commercially known as Falcon Security (Seguridad Falcon), is a company founded at the end of 2012 in Guanacaste. We were registered, and authorized in April of 2013 by the Ministry of Public Safety, within the division of ‘Private Security’. Falcon Security holds the following licenses No. 0075- 2013DSSP and No. 00262014DDSSP, which qualifies and authorizes us to legally exercise business activities specialized in electronic security and massive events security.

ABOUT US

Falcon maintains it’s beginnings and headquarters in the colonial city of Nicoya, which makes this company the first private electronic security company with a local presence to offer electronic security products and services along with armed guard response services. We strive and focus on innovation, research, and development of new integral security solutions. Our goal is to fully communicate with the end-user so that their needs become the source of inspiration and further reason of improvement.

Our growing business has created competitiveness in the current marketplace. We have made this a reality thanks to a significant increase in sales within all our business lines and thanks to the continuous search for improvement in management efficiency. The result allows us to offer our clients competitive prices, timely product support, and needs-oriented services. The objective of our company is to continue growing at a sustained pace, therefore, reinforcing our leadership in the electronic security sector for our customers’ benefit .

OUR MISSION

Provide comprehensive security and protection solutions by delivering services and products of the highest quality to our cus- tomers, and encouraging our employees to develop their profession- al skills, offering a growing and sustainable profitability to our shareholders and of course, being respectful with the environ- ment.

OUR VISION

To be an exemplary company at the national level, and especial- ly in the Guanacaste region, related to residential, commercial, vehicular and corporate security and protection, all the while rigorously applying our positive values, ethical work philosophy, and characterizing ourselves as providers of integral security solu- tions of the highest technological quality for our customers benefit.
